Post-Matric Scholarship for Students with Disabilities

Awards:

  • Maintenance Allowance: Up to Rs. 1600 per month for day scholars and Rs. 750 per month for hostellers.
  • Book Grant: Rs. 1500 per annum for undergraduate studies and Rs. 2000 per annum for postgraduate studies.
  • Tuition Fee: Up to Rs. 1.50 lakh per annum.
  • Disability Allowance: Rs. 2000 per annum for persons with disability ranging from 40% to 60% and Rs. 4000 per annum for persons with disability of 80% and above.
  • Additional benefits: Escort Allowance, Reader Allowance, Computer/Laptop with accessories (one-time grant), Aids and Assistive devices (one-time grant).

Eligibility:

  • Indian national with at least 40% disability as certified by a competent authority.
  • Pursuing Class XI to post-graduation in any university/institution recognized by UGC/AICTE.
  • Annual family income from all sources less than Rs. 2.5 lakh.

Application Fees:

  • Nil - No application fees are required for this scholarship.

Application Procedure:

  1. Apply online through the National Scholarship Portal (https://scholarships.gov.in/).
  2. Register and fill the application form with all required documents.
  3. Submit the application before the deadline.

Selection Process:

  1. Applications are initially reviewed by designated State Nodal agencies.
  2. Shortlisted candidates are called for document verification.
  3. Based on eligibility, merit, and disability, scholarships are awarded.

Other Information:

  • The scheme is implemented by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD), Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India.
  • For detailed guidelines and updates, refer to the official website of DEPwD (https://scholarships.gov.in/public/schemeGuidelines/DEPDGuidelines_1.pdf).

Important Dates:

  • Applications for the 2023-24 academic year are usually open from September to November. However, the exact dates for the current year may vary.
  • It's advisable to check the official website or contact your state Nodal agency for the latest information.
