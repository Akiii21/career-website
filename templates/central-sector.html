Central Sector Scheme of Scholarships for College and University Students

Awards:

  • Fresh scholarships: 82,000 awarded annually, equally divided between boys and girls.
  • Amount:
    • Rs. 10,000 per annum for the first three years of undergraduate studies (due to a temporary decrease, as of Oct 2023).
    • Rs. 12,000 per annum for the first three years after the temporary decrease is lifted.
    • Rs. 20,000 per annum for the fourth and fifth years in professional courses.
    • Rs. 20,000 per annum for postgraduate studies.

Eligibility:

  • Indian nationals only.
  • Students who scored above the 80th percentile in their Class 12th board exams in Humanities, Science, or Commerce stream.
  • Pursuing regular undergraduate, postgraduate, or professional courses (engineering, medical, etc.) in recognized universities/institutions.
  • Annual family income from all sources less than Rs. 4.5 lakh.

Application Fees:

  • Nil - No application fees are required for this scholarship.

Application Procedure:

  1. Apply online through the National Scholarship Portal (https://scholarships.gov.in/).
  2. Registration and fill the application form with all required documents.
  3. Submit the application before the deadline.

Selection Process:

  1. Applications are initially reviewed by State Education Boards based on eligibility criteria.
  2. Shortlisted candidates are called for document verification.
  3. Scholarships are awarded based on merit (Class 12th percentile score) and stream-wise allocation quotas.

Other Information:

  • The scheme is administered by the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India.
  • Scholarship payments are made directly to beneficiaries' bank accounts through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).
  • Renewal for subsequent years is possible based on continued academic performance and meeting income eligibility criteria.
  • For detailed guidelines and latest updates, refer to the official website of the scheme (https://scholarships.gov.in/).

Important Dates:

  • Applications for the 2023-24 academic year are typically open from October to November.
  • However, it's advisable to check the official website or your State Education Board for the most recent deadlines and updates.
