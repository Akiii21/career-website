Prime Minister's Scholarship Scheme

Awards:

  • Monthly Maintenance Allowance: Rs. 2,500 per month for boys and Rs. 3,000 per month for girls.
  • Book Grant: Rs. 1,500 per annum for undergraduate studies and Rs. 2,000 per annum for postgraduate studies.
  • Tuition Fee Reimbursement: Up to Rs. 2 lakh per annum for undergraduate studies and Rs. 3 lakh per annum for postgraduate studies.
  • Additional Benefits: Laptop/Computer with accessories (one-time grant), Thesis grant (one-time grant) for postgraduate students only.

Eligibility:

  • Candidate must be an Indian citizen.
  • Candidate must be a widow or ward of a deceased/ex-servicemen/ex-Coast Guard personnel (below officer rank) who died in harness, due to causes attributable to service, or are disabled due to such causes.
  • Candidate must be pursuing studies in a full-time first year of a technical course in Medical, Dental, Veterinary, Engineering, MBA, MCA, or any other equivalent technical institutions approved by AICTE/UGC.
  • Annual family income from all sources must not exceed Rs. 8.00 lakh per annum.

Application Fees:

  • Nil - No application fees are required for this scholarship.

Application Procedure:

  1. Apply online through the National Scholarship Portal (https://scholarships.gov.in/).
  2. Register and fill the application form with all required documents before the deadline.
  3. The exact timeline is announced annually, typically from around September to November.

Selection Process:

  1. Applications are initially reviewed by designated State Nodal agencies.
  2. Shortlisted candidates are called for document verification.
  3. Based on eligibility, merit, and specific criteria for wards/widows, scholarships are awarded.

Other Information:

  • The scheme is implemented by the Department of Ex-servicemen Welfare, Ministry of Defence, Government of India.
  • For detailed guidelines and updates, refer to the official website of the Department of Ex-servicemen Welfare (https://www.educationupdates.org/pm-scholarship-scheme-pmss-desw-gov-in/).

Important Dates:

  • The application deadline for the 2023-24 academic year is likely to be announced soon, usually around October/November 2023.
  • It's advisable to check the official website or contact your state Nodal agency for the latest information.
