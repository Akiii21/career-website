Scholarships to Young Artists in Different Cultural Fields (SYA)

Awards:

  • Monthly stipend: Rs. 5,000 for two years, paid in six-month installments.
  • Training expenses: Covers tuition fees, travel, books, art materials, and other equipment, if any.

Eligibility:

  • Age between 18 and 25 years.
  • Indian national with a minimum of five years of training under a recognized Guru/Institution in the chosen field.
  • Demonstrated talent and potential for advanced training in one of the following fields:
    • Indian Classical Music (Vocal & Instrumental - Hindustani and Carnatic)
    • Indian Classical Dance (Bharatanatyam, Odissi, Kathak, Kuchipudi, Mohiniattam, Manipuri)
    • Theatre (Acting, Playwriting, Directing)
    • Mime
    • Visual Arts (Painting, Sculpture, Graphics, Printmaking)
    • Folk, Traditional and Indigenous Arts
    • Light Classical Music

Application Fees:

  • Nil - No application fees are required for this scholarship.

Application Procedure:

  1. Applications are accepted online through the official website of the Ministry of Culture (https://www.indiaculture.gov.in/).
  2. The application form and guidelines are available on the website.
  3. Applications are usually open from August to October.

Selection Process:

  1. Applications are reviewed by a committee of experts in the respective fields.
  2. Shortlisted candidates are called for an interview and practical demonstration of their skills.
  3. Scholarships are awarded based on talent, potential, and need.

Other Information:

  • The program is intended to provide financial assistance and mentorship to young artists, enabling them to hone their skills and contribute to the preservation and promotion of Indian culture.
  • For detailed guidelines and updates, refer to the official website mentioned above.

Important Dates:

  • The exact dates for the next application cycle will be announced by the Ministry of Culture closer to the deadline. However, as mentioned earlier, applications typically open in August and close in October.
  • It's advisable to check the official website or contact the Ministry of Culture for the latest information.
