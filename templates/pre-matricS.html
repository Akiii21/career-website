Pre-Matric Scholarship for Students with Disabilities

Awards:

  • Maintenance Allowance: Up to Rs. 1000 per month for day scholars and Rs. 500 per month for hostellers.
  • Book Grant: Rs. 1000 per annum.
  • Disability Allowance:
    • Visually Impaired or Intellectual Disabilities: Rs. 4000 per annum.
    • Other types of disabilities: Rs. 2000 per annum.

Eligibility:

  • Indian national with at least 40% disability as certified by a competent authority.
  • Enrolled in Class 9 or 10 in a government school or a school recognized by the government or CBSE/State Board.
  • Annual family income from all sources less than Rs. 2.5 lakh.

Application Fees:

  • Nil - No application fees are required for this scholarship.

Application Procedure:

  1. Apply online through the National Scholarship Portal ( https://scholarships.gov.in/).
  2. Complete the application form and submit it with required documents before the deadline.

Selection Process:

  1. Applications are initially screened by State/UT Nodal Agencies.
  2. Shortlisted candidates are considered for scholarship based on disability, merit, and income.

Other Information:

  • The scheme is implemented by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD), Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India.
  • For detailed guidelines and updates, refer to the official website of DEPwD ( https://scholarships.gov.in/public/schemeGuidelines/DEPDGuidelines.pdf) or your State/UT Nodal Agency.

Important Dates:

  • Applications for the 2023-24 academic year are usually open from October to November. However, the exact dates may vary.
  • It's advisable to check the official website or contact your State/UT Nodal agency for the latest information.
