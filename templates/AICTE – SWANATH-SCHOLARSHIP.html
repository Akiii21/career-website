AICTE – SWANATH SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME

Awards:

  • Annual amount: Rs. 50,000 per year, for a maximum of 4 years for Degree students and 3 years for Diploma students.
  • Lump sum payment: The scholarship is provided as a lump sum amount towards payment of college fees, purchase of computers, stationery, books, equipment, software, etc.

Eligibility:

  • Must belong to one of the above-mentioned categories (orphan, Covid-19 orphan, ward of martyred personnel).
  • Enrolled in the first year of a full-time technical degree or diploma course at an AICTE-approved institution.
  • Passed the qualifying examination (10th or 12th) with at least 60% marks.
  • Annual family income from all sources less than Rs. 6 lakh.

Application Fees:

  • Nil - No application fees are required for this scholarship.

Application Procedure:

  1. Applications are invited online through the National Scholarship Portal (https://scholarships.gov.in/).
  2. Registration and form completion are required before the deadline.
  3. Necessary documents such as income certificate, disability certificate (if applicable), etc., must be uploaded.

Selection Process:

  1. Applications are initially screened based on eligibility criteria.
  2. Shortlisted candidates may be called for document verification.
  3. Scholarships are awarded based on merit and adherence to eligibility criteria.

Other Information:

  • The scheme is implemented through designated State Nodal Agencies.
  • More details and guidelines are available on the AICTE website (https://www.aicte-india.org/sites/default/files/stdc/AICTE-SWANATH-%20SCHEME%20final.pdf).

Important Dates:

  • Applications for the 2023-24 academic year may have closed, as the deadline is usually in November.
  • Check the AICTE website or your state Nodal agency for upcoming application periods and updates.
